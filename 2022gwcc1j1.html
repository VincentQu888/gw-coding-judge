2022 G.W. Coding Contest 1 J1: Message coder

You work for a top-secret government agency and must send coded messages to your colleagues. Write a program that writes a coded message.

Here's how the message coder should work.

  • Every 5-letter or longer word should have its letters reversed. i.e. "pokemon" corresponds to "nomekop"
  • Every 4 letter or fewer words should keep only their first and last letters.
  • 1 letter words remain the same

    • Sample Input:

    pokemon are cool

    Sample Output:

    nomekop ae cl
