2022 G.W. Coding Contest 1 S1: Semimagic Squares:

Magic squares are grids with the numbers in each column, each row, and each corner-to-corner diagonal adding up to the same total.

However, a semi-magic square is a magic square, but with the diagonals adding up to a different sum.

Given the length l and width w of a grid, and the grid of numbers, print "yes" if the square is semi-magic, and print "no" if it is not.


Sample Input:

4

16 2 3 13

5 11 10 8

9 7 6 12

4 14 15 1


Sample Output:

no
